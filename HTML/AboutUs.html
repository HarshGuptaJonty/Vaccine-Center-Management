About Us

Hello user! We are three college students pursuing a BCA degree from UEM, Kolkata. Currently, we are in our 2nd year. VCM is our Web Design Project. We are glad that you choose our services, if you face any problems or have any suggestions, feel free to contact us. Thank you.

We got this project idea because of this recent pandemic. In this pandemic, we saw many vaccination centers facing problems while handling their clients data. Centers placed in rural areas faced obstacles while connecting to the Government vaccination portal because of the vast network traffic. We came up with this idea to assist them, so the vaccination process continues without any difficulties.
